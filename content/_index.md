title: "Martin Buchert"

I'm a **geographer** and **conservation planner** with an inside perspective on the **built environment industries** that conservation work is always negotiating against. From **habitat connectivity modeling in US tallgrass prairie remnants** to **land titling operations across Sub-Saharan Africa** to **aerial surveys in the Guianan forests of Amazonia,** my field and research experience spans four continents across nine ecosystem types and the full spectrum from **wildlands to intensely designed urban systems**.

Along the way I've secured **grant and contract funding**, led **multi-disciplinary field programs** across multiple countries, and built the **teams and institutional partnerships** that turn missions into operational results — from building my own **GIS consultancy** to captaining proposal efforts that won **international survey** and **land governance contracts** at Medici Land Governance. I taught GIS at the graduate level for a decade at the University of Utah.
