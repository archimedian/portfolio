I'm a **geographer**, **conservation planner**, and someone who has spent significant time inside the **built environment industries** that conservation work is always negotiating against. My work has taken me from **aerial surveys in the Congo Basin** to **land titling operations in Amazonian Guyana** to **eelgrass mapping in Puget Sound** — across nine ecosystem types, five continents, and the full spectrum from **wildlands to intensely designed urban systems**.

Along the way I've built the **teams**, **partnerships**, and **funding relationships** that make that kind of work possible in the first place.
